# App Brainstorming

## Initial Ideas

### 1. 🎓 Swipe-Based College Discovery App *(CollegeCling / Univunity)*
A Tinder-style app where students swipe through college profiles to discover schools and apply. Personalized by GPA, interests, location, and budget. Targets high school seniors, transfer students, and grad school applicants who are overwhelmed by the college search process.

### 2. 💸 Student Budget Tracker
A budgeting app tailored to college students that categorizes spending (tuition, dining, textbooks, fun), links to a meal plan or campus card, and gives weekly spending reports. Includes alerts when nearing budget limits for specific categories.

### 3. 🏠 Off-Campus Housing Matcher
An app that helps college students find off-campus housing AND compatible roommates at the same time. Users fill out a lifestyle profile (sleep schedule, cleanliness, noise level) and are matched with listings + potential roommates who fit.

### 4. 📅 College Event & Club Discovery
A centralized hub for campus events, club meetings, and social activities. Students follow clubs, get push notifications for events, RSVP, and see a live campus activity feed. Solves the problem of events being scattered across flyers, Instagram, and email listservs.

### 5. 🎯 Internship & Opportunity Swipe Board
A swipe-based internship and scholarship discovery app for college students. Users set a profile with their major, skills, and interests and swipe through curated opportunities. One-tap apply or save for later, with deadline reminders.

### 6. 🧠 Flashcard & Study Tool with AI
An AI-powered study app where students upload their notes or syllabi and the app auto-generates flashcards, practice quizzes, and summaries. Tracks performance over time and suggests what to review before exams.

## Evaluation of Top 3 Ideas

### 🥇 1. Swipe-Based College Discovery App
| Criteria | Evaluation |
|----------|------------|
| **Mobile** | ✅ Swipe interactions are inherently mobile-native — perfect fit |
| **Story** | ✅ Strong narrative: students are overwhelmed; this makes the process fast and simple |
| **Market** | ✅ ~20M+ students apply to college in the US annually — massive addressable market |
| **Habit** | ✅ High engagement during application season (Aug–Jan); users return daily to swipe and check status |
| **Scope** | ✅ Core MVP (login, swipe, save, apply) is achievable; advanced features are extensible |

**Verdict:** Best overall balance of marketability, uniqueness, and buildability. Strong visual identity and clear user value.

---

### 🥈 2. Internship & Opportunity Swipe Board
| Criteria | Evaluation |
|----------|------------|
| **Mobile** | ✅ Swipe-based, great for quick browsing on the go |
| **Story** | ✅ Real pain point — students miss opportunities because job boards are cluttered and overwhelming |
| **Market** | ✅ Every college student is a potential user; internship hunting is universal |
| **Habit** | ⚠️ Moderate — mostly used during recruiting seasons (fall/spring), not year-round |
| **Scope** | ⚠️ Requires integrating job listing data; slightly more backend complexity |

**Verdict:** Very strong idea but overlaps heavily with LinkedIn and Handshake. Harder to differentiate.

---

### 🥉 3. Off-Campus Housing Matcher
| Criteria | Evaluation |
|----------|------------|
| **Mobile** | ✅ Good fit for mobile browsing and messaging |
| **Story** | ✅ Finding roommates and housing simultaneously solves a real dual-problem |
| **Market** | ✅ Relevant to most upperclassmen and transfer students |
| **Habit** | ❌ Low — used intensely for a few weeks per year, then abandoned |
| **Scope** | ⚠️ Safety, verification, and listing management add significant complexity |

**Verdict:** Creative and useful, but low retention and high complexity make it harder to execute well in a limited timeframe.

---

## ✅ Final App Idea: Swipe-Based College Discovery & Application App

**Working Name:** **Univunity** *(University + Unity)*

Our final choice is the swipe-based college discovery and application platform. It best satisfies all five evaluation criteria, offers a clear and emotionally resonant user story, and has a realistic MVP scope. The core interaction model — swiping through personalized college profiles — is intuitive, mobile-native, and uniquely positions the app against traditional college search tools. It empowers students to navigate one of the most stressful decisions of their lives with confidence and ease.
